Amphitheater

The PUP Amphitheater is an open-air performance venue situated within the PUP Lagoon which serves as a space for various outdoor events such theatrical performances.

Obelisk

The Obelisk at the Polytechnic University of the Philippines symbolizes the strength and values of the institution. It represents their commitment to education, morality, and leadership, supported by a dedicated system of public service.

Interfaith Chapel

Interfaith Ecumenical Chapel is a place of worship in Metro Manila. PUP Interfaith Ecumenical Chapel is situated nearby to Boulder Fountain and the obelisk Flag Pole Hoist Area.

Lagoon

The Lagoon, for some, was just an ordinary mossy creek with trees around and birds chirping for drama; but this is something special for every PUPian. This was probably the most hung-out place compared to the rest of the spot in PUP.

Pasig River

The Pasig River is a water body in the Philippines that connects Laguna de Bay to Manila Bay.

Swimming Pool

An olympic-sized swimming pool that is capable of handling several swimming classes and student athlete activities simultaneously.

Main Building

The Polytechnic University of the Philippines (PUP) Main Building is the central and iconic structure of the PUP campus located in Manila, Philippines.

PUP Track and Field Oval

The PUP Track and Field Oval is a spacious outdoor sports facility located within the university campus. It serves as a dedicated area for various athletic activities, particularly track and field events.

Ninoy Aquino Library and Learning Resources Center

The Ninoy Aquino Library and Learning Resources Center is the library system of the Polytechnic University of the Philippines composed of libraries providing services to the PUP System.

Pasig River Ferry (Sta. Mesa Station)

Located in PUP, the Pasig River Ferry Station in Sta. Mesa is one of the designated stops along the Pasig River Ferry route. The station serves as a boarding and disembarking point for students, faculy, and other passengers traveling via the ferry system.
